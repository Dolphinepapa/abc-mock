## Part 0 — Cross-cutting infrastructure (build first)

### 0.1 `<MetricTerm>` hover tooltip primitive

Build a reusable component that wraps any jargon-y metric label and shows a tooltip on hover.

**Usage**:
```jsx
<MetricTerm definition="Attach rate = % of buyers of the primary product (razor) who purchase the complementary product (blade) within 90 days. Calculated from Amazon Brand Analytics Repeat Purchase Behavior.">
  Attach rate
</MetricTerm>: 47%
```

**Visual**:
- Wrapped text gets a subtle dotted underline (1px dashed slate-400)
- On hover: tooltip appears above (or below if no room), slate-900 background, white text, max-width 280px, soft shadow, rounded-md, small arrow pointer
- Position: hovers near the term, doesn't get cut off by container

**Definitions to ship (centralize in a `METRIC_DEFINITIONS` map at top of file)**:

- **Attach rate**: % of buyers of a primary product who purchase the complementary product within a defined window. (Computed from Amazon Brand Analytics Repeat Purchase Behavior.)
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value): Total expected contribution margin from a customer over their lifetime with the brand.
- **Blended margin**: Weighted average of product margins across an entire product line.
- **TACoS**: Total Advertising Cost of Sales. Ad spend ÷ total sales (organic + ad-attributed).
- **ACoS**: Advertising Cost of Sales. Ad spend ÷ ad-attributed sales only.
- **Contribution margin**: Sale price minus all variable costs (COGS, fulfillment, unit-attributable ad spend).
- **SOV** (Share of Voice): A brand's share of total impressions on a defined keyword cluster.
- **Cohort revenue**: Revenue generated by a defined group of customers (e.g., those acquired in May) over a specified time window.
- **Cost Cap bidding**: TikTok bidding strategy where you set a target cost-per-action; the platform optimizes to stay near it (not guaranteed; no refund if missed).
- **Incrementality**: The causal contribution of a channel to revenue beyond what would have happened without it. Measured via geographic holdout testing.
- **Geographic holdout test**: Run ads in a set of regions while withholding in matched control regions, then compare sales to isolate true causal impact.
- **CTR**: Click-through rate. Clicks ÷ impressions.
- **CR**: Conversion rate. Orders ÷ clicks.
